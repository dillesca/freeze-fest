Freeze Fest Game Rules

Quick reference for KanJam, Cornhole, Bucket Golf, and Beersbee play at {{ event.name }}.

KanJam

Two-player teams take turns throwing and deflecting a flying disc toward a goal can.

  • Teams alternate throws; partners stand at opposite cans and swap roles (thrower/deflector) each toss.
  • Scoring: redirect onto the can = 1 point, direct hit on can = 2, disc through slot/top without help = instant win.
  • No stepping past the front of the can when throwing. Deflectors may not grab or carry the disc.
  • Play to 21. If both teams hit 21 in the same round, continue until one leads after an equal number of turns.

Cornhole (First to 11)

Classic bag toss where teams race to exactly eleven points—go over and you drop back to seven.

  • Partners line up at opposite boards. Each player tosses four bags per frame, alternating throws.
  • Scoring uses cancellation: bag on the board = 1 point, through the hole = 3 points.
  • First team to hit 11 points wins. If you bust (score above 11), your total resets to 7 and play continues.

Bucket Golf (6 Holes)

A short walking golf course with yard obstacles and buckets for holes.

  • Each hole has a tee marker and a target bucket. Play the course in order for all six holes.
  • Only the grass is in bounds. Anything landing on mulch, pavement, or patios is out of bounds—take a one-stroke penalty and continue from the previous lie.
  • Touching the outside of the bucket finishes the hole. If you drop the ball into the bucket, subtract one stroke from your total for that hole.
  • Both partners play each hole independently; record the better of the two scores for the team.
  • Ties for playoff seeding or advancement are settled by sudden-death extra holes starting from hole one.

Beersbee (Finale)

A frisbee face-off where accuracy and quick reflexes earn the Freeze Fest trophy.

  • Teams stand behind their poles (or stakes) set about 30 feet apart with a bottle perched on top.
  • Throwers aim to knock the bottle off the opponent’s pole; teammates must stay behind their line until release.
  • Scoring: bottle knocked cleanly = 2 points, pole hit and bottle caught before touching ground = 1 point to defense, dropped bottle = 1 point to offense.
  • Play to 11, win by 2. If tied at 10–10, alternate throws until one team leads after an equal number of turns.

Have a question mid-match? Flag an organizer and keep the freeze going.
